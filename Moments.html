Notable Information

Historic Champions League Run

2016-2018

Crisitano Ronaldo and Real Madrid went on a historic Champions League run over a 3 year time period. In that time, he and his team won the Champions League 3 times in a row, both the first team to retain the title in the Champions League era, and also the first team to win the trophy 3 times in a row in the Champions League era.

Spectacular Season

2014-15

At Real Madrid, Cristiano Ronaldo had his best indiviudal seasons in his career. Although not domestically successful, only winning the Fifa Club World Cup and UEFA Super Cup while falling short in the league and other Spanish compeitions, Ronaldo managed to score a career high 61 goals in the entirety of the 2014-2015 season.

Unforgettable Goal

vs Juventus, 2018

In the semi-finals of the Champions League, Real Madrid and Crisitano Ronaldo were faced with the challenge of defeating Juventus of Italy. In the big occasion, Crisitano Ronaldo stepped up to the challenge and produced a goal that was deserving of being hanged in the Louvre. Ronaldo scored a bicylcle kick that is with out a doubt the best goal of his career and widely considered the greatest goal of all time.

National Hero

Euro 2016

France and Germany being the major favorites to win the Euros, Cristiano Ronaldo and his Portugal side were widely considered underdogs to the tournament with the team struggling in recent fixtures. Nonetheless, Cristiano Ronaldo carried his nation to the finals of the Euros, with him unfortunately getting injured early into the final. In the end, Cristiano Ronaldo and Portugal came out victorious over hosts France thanks to Ronaldo's coaching efforts on the sidelines, winning the nations first major trophy in its history.
